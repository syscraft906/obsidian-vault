# Where Are Conversation Designers Now?

## AI Summary
This blog post explores the career trajectories and challenges of conversation designers across three historical waves (1980s-2009, 2010–2019, 2020–now). The author analyzed 146 LinkedIn profiles to uncover patterns in career progression, title evolution, and employment trends. Key findings include the lack of clear career ladders in conversation design, the persistence of designers in the field despite challenges, and the growing title fragmentation in recent years. The post emphasizes that while there’s no standardized path, most designers remain in design-related roles long-term.
