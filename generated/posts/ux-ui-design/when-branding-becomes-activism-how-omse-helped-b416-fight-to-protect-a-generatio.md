# When branding becomes activism: how OMSE helped B416 fight to protect a generation

## AI Summary
This post highlights how design studio OMSE partnered with the B416 campaign to create a powerful brand that influenced legislation, raising the minimum age for social media in New Zealand. By leveraging familiar visual language like content ratings and strategic messaging, OMSE transformed B416 from a grassroots initiative into a credible movement that secured a government bill. The collaboration combined design, photography, and interactive tools to advocate for policy change, demonstrating how effective branding can drive real societal impact.
