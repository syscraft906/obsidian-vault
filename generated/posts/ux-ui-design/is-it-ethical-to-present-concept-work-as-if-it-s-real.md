# Is it ethical to present concept work as if it's real?

## AI Summary
The blog post explores the ethical implications of presenting speculative or concept design work as real client projects in portfolios. It highlights debates among designers about the value of concept work for showcasing creativity and learning, while emphasizing the importance of honesty in labeling such projects. The article concludes that while concept work can be useful, especially for emerging designers, it becomes unethical when mislabeled as actual client work. Key takeaways stress transparency and the distinction between concept and real-world projects in professional portfolios.
