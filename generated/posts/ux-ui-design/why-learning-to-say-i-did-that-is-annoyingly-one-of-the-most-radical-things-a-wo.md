# Why learning to say 'I did that' is annoyingly one of the most radical things a woman can do

## AI Summary
The blog post discusses Galaxy's 'The Unhumble Project' campaign, which challenges the cultural expectation for women to downplay their achievements. The author shares a personal journey of self-reflection after a back injury, realizing the importance of recognizing her own accomplishments without shrinking them. The campaign positions confidence as a learnable skill rather than an innate trait, offering practical tools to help women celebrate their successes and avoid the 'modesty trap' that limits visibility, pay equity, and opportunities.
