# Designing For Agentic AI: Practical UX Patterns For Control, Consent, And Accountability

## AI Summary
This article outlines six practical UX patterns for designing agentic AI systems that prioritize user control, transparency, and trust. It details patterns likeIntent Preview, Autonomy Dial, and Action Audit & Undo, each with implementation guidance, psychological principles, and metrics for success. The post emphasizes governance structures, escalation pathways, and a phased rollout strategy for product teams to safely integrate agentic AI while maintaining user accountability.
