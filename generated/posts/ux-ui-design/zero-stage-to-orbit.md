# Zero Stage to Orbit

## AI Summary
This article draws parallels between multi-stage rocket engineering and the traditional design-to-development pipeline, arguing that excessive coordination and translation overhead plague modern workflows. It critiques the 'gravity well' of stage-based processes and proposes AI-powered tools as a solution to eliminate handoffs, enabling direct intent-to-execution workflows. The author advocates moving 'from orbit'—reducing translation layers while maintaining the importance of specialized skills like research and architecture.
