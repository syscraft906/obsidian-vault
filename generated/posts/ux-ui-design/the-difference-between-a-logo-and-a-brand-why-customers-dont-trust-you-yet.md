# The Difference Between a Logo and a Brand: Why Customers Don’t Trust You Yet

## AI Summary
This post explains the critical difference between a logo and a brand, emphasizing that a logo is a visual identifier while a brand represents a business's full identity, values, and customer experience. It highlights how small businesses often confuse the two, leading to inconsistencies that harm trust and growth. The article stresses the need for a cohesive brand identity—including tone, visuals, and reputation—to build customer loyalty and stand out in competitive markets.
