# From Symbols to Systems: Designing brands that hold under pressure

## AI Summary
The blog post emphasizes designing resilient brand systems by anchoring them to essential elements rather than overloading them with rigid rules. It argues that in a fast-evolving cultural and technological landscape, brands must balance core consistency with flexibility, using AI and new media to pressure-test their identity. Key strategies include focusing on business-culture alignment, defining a clear core through symbols like logos and typography, modulating intensity ('volume control') in different contexts, and fostering collaborative growth with skilled internal teams.
