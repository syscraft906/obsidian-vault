# How To Measure The Impact Of Features

## AI Summary
This blog introduces the TARS framework (Target Audience, Adoption, Retention, Satisfaction) for measuring the impact of product features. It explains how to quantify each component, from identifying the target audience percentage to assessing feature adoption, retention rates, and user satisfaction scores. The post also critiques conversion rate as a UX metric, emphasizing the importance of UX-specific metrics for accurate impact analysis.
