# Designing A Streak System: The UX And Psychology Of Streaks

## AI Summary
The blog post explores the psychology and UX design of streak systems in apps, highlighting their power to drive user engagement through principles like loss aversion, the Fogg Behaviour Model (B=MAP), and the Zeigarnik effect. It emphasizes ethical design that encourages habits without inducing compulsion, covering UX strategies such as effortless actions, visual feedback, timely prompts, milestone celebrations, and grace mechanisms for missed days. Technical challenges like timezone handling, edge cases, and cheating prevention are outlined, with architectural recommendations for robust implementation.
