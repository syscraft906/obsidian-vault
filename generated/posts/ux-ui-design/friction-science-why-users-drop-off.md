# Friction Science: Why Users Drop Off

## AI Summary
This article explores why users abandon digital experiences, emphasizing that drop-offs stem from psychological friction rather than purely design flaws. It breaks down four types of friction—cognitive, emotional, behavioral, and interaction—and how they compound into a 'friction stack.' Backed by research from MIT, Stanford, and behavioral science, the post argues that reducing transactional barriers and aligning design with mental models and emotional states is key to improving user retention. Examples show how reordering steps or clarifying options can significantly reduce abandonment.
