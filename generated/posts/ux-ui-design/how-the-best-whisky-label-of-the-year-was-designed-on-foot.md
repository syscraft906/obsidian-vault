# How the best whisky label of the year was designed on foot

## AI Summary
This post explores how design agency Thirst created East London Whisky's label by prioritizing urban observation over digital references. By walking through East London and capturing authentic textures and typography, they disrupted traditional whisky packaging conventions to create a tactile, place-driven design. The approach highlights the value of on-the-ground research in crafting meaningful brand narratives that balance heritage and innovation.
