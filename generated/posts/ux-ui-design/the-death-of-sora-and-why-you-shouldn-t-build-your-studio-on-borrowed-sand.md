# The death of Sora, and why you shouldn't build your studio on borrowed sand

## AI Summary
The blog post discusses the sudden shutdown of OpenAI's Sora video generation tool and highlights risks for creatives relying on unstable AI platforms. It emphasizes how financial pressures drove Sora's discontinuation, leaving users and partners like Disney in limbo. The author warns against over-dependent workflows on single AI tools due to unpredictable business models and evolving legal frameworks, urging professionals to maintain portable skills and avoid load-bearing reliance on unproven platforms.
