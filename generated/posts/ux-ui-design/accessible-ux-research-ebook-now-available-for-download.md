# Accessible UX Research, eBook Now Available For Download

## AI Summary
This blog post announces the release of 'Accessible UX Research,' an eBook exploring how to conduct inclusive UX research that accommodates users with disabilities. The book emphasizes practical strategies for integrating accessibility into every stage of research, from planning to reporting, and challenges common misconceptions about disability. Written by accessibility expert Michele A. Williams, it serves as a roadmap for UX professionals, designers, and product teams seeking to create more equitable user experiences. The content highlights actionable guidance on recruitment, facilitation, and ethical considerations while moving beyond compliance to foster true inclusion.
