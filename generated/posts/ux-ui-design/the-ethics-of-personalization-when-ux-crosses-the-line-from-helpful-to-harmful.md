# The Ethics of Personalization: When UX Crosses the Line from Helpful to Harmful

## AI Summary
This article explores the ethical challenges of personalization in UX design, highlighting how hyper-personalization can lead to echo chambers, biased algorithms, and mental health risks. It uses examples like Facebook's newsfeed and TikTok's content recommendations to illustrate the societal and psychological impacts of over-filtering user experiences. The post argues for transparency, diversity, bias audits, and well-being checkpoints in ethical personalization practices to preserve user autonomy and prevent harmful outcomes.
