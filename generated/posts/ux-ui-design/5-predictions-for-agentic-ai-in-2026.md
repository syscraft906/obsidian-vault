# 5 Predictions for Agentic AI in 2026

## AI Summary
This blog post highlights five predictions for agentic AI in 2026 based on insights from Robb Wilson and Josh Tyson of Invisible Machines. The discussion covers trends like the rise of consumer-accessible outbound AI and the adoption of agent runtime environments in organizations. It suggests that agentic AI will become a baseline expectation for businesses rather than a competitive edge, and outlines the potential impacts on operations, investment strategies, and consumer interactions in the coming year.
