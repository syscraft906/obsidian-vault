# Why ChatGPT-Style Answers are the Future of Site Search and How to Add It to Your Website

## AI Summary
The blog post explores how AI-driven conversational search, akin to ChatGPT, is transforming user expectations by providing direct answers rather than mere links. It highlights challenges with using general AI models for this purpose, such as hallucinations and data privacy risks, and presents AddSearch as a secure, content-centric alternative. AddSearch integrates AI-generated answers and multi-turn conversations with existing search systems, offering customization of tone and transparency while avoiding third-party data sharing. The post emphasizes the importance of this shift for improving user experience and maintaining compliance in AI implementation.
