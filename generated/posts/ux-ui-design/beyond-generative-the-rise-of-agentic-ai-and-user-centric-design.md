# Beyond Generative: The Rise Of Agentic AI And User-Centric Design

## AI Summary
The blog explores Agentic AI's evolution beyond generative models, emphasizing its ability to autonomously plan, execute, and adapt tasks. It distinguishes Agentic AI from RPA and predictive systems through examples like meeting conflict resolution and recruiting workflows. Key concepts include four autonomy levels (Observed-and-Suggest to Act-Autonomously), research methodologies for trust-building design, ethical considerations, and performance metrics like intervention rates. The post advocates for transparency, user control, and proactive UX research to navigate AI's shifting role from tool to autonomous partner.
