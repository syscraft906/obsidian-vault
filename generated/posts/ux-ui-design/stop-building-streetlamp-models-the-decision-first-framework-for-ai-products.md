# Stop Building Streetlamp Models: The Decision-First Framework for AI Products

## AI Summary
This article discusses common failures in AI product development due to misaligned priorities, emphasizing the 'Streetlamp Trap' where teams build models based on available data rather than user needs. It introduces the AI-Question Framework—three critical questions to ensure AI projects address real business metrics, leverage historical data, and account for tolerable errors. The decision-first approach prioritizes user and business impact over technical feasibility, offering practical strategies for product teams to avoid analytics theater and drive value. Examples from EdTech illustrate how to refocus AI projects on actionable outcomes like retention and revenue.
