# How behavioral science can help persuade our team to do one more user test

## AI Summary
This post explores how behavioral science principles can be applied to encourage cross-functional teams to prioritize user testing. It addresses common challenges designers face in slowing down product development to conduct research, and offers practical strategies based on concepts like social proof, loss aversion, and commitment devices. The key value lies in actionable methods to align team incentives with user-centered goals, fostering better collaboration and research adoption.
