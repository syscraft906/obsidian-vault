# Unhappy Agile Teams Are Unhappy in Familiar Ways

## AI Summary
The blog post explores common anti-patterns in Agile teams, such as AgileFall (hybrid Waterfall-Agile practices), treating design as a service, feature factories, and complete chaos. It emphasizes that these issues arise from a misunderstanding or misapplication of Agile principles, prioritizing rituals over collaboration and real user needs. The author highlights the importance of cross-functional teamwork, shared direction, and balancing speed with meaningful iteration to avoid these traps and achieve genuine Agile success.
