# Why AI Scaffolding Matters More than Use Cases

## AI Summary
This blog discusses the critical importance of building foundational AI scaffolding—like agent runtime infrastructure—instead of prioritizing flashy use cases in enterprise AI adoption. It highlights how organizational gaps, not technology, often lead to AI project failures and offers strategies for scaling AI initiatives through human-centered design and sustainable deployment. The post features insights from Erika Flowers, an expert in AI readiness, on preparing for a 'post-software' AI-driven future.
