# Combobox vs. Multiselect vs. Listbox: How To Choose The Right One

## AI Summary
This article explains the differences between combobox, multiselect, listbox, and dropdown UI components, focusing on their use cases based on the number of options, visibility, and interactivity. It highlights when to use each component—such as listboxes for visible lists, combobox for type-to-filter single selection, and multiselect for multiple choices—and emphasizes accessibility, usability, and keyboard navigation principles. The post also provides guidelines for avoiding clutter while ensuring frequent options remain accessible.
