# What AI exposes about design

## AI Summary
The blog post explores how AI is transforming design by automating UI/UX tasks, shifting focus back to core design principles like user empathy and systemic problem-solving. It critiques the over-specialization of design disciplines, loss of strategic impact, and reliance on superficial design trends. The author proposes leveraging AI for rapid prototyping and testing while emphasizing the irreplaceable role of human-centered insights. Concepts like 'Design Twin' are introduced to maintain empathy in AI-driven workflows, warning against static data reliance and advocating for continuous human observation.
