# UNESCO - Virtual Museum of Stolen Cultural Objects

## AI Summary
The UNESCO Virtual Museum of Stolen Cultural Objects showcases digitally restored artifacts to highlight their historical and cultural significance while advocating for repatriation. The platform aims to raise awareness about looted heritage, support provenance research, and inform restitution efforts. Visitors can explore interactive exhibits that emphasize the absence of these items from their original contexts.
