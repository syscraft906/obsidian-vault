# The design engineer symptom: what a rising job title reveals

## AI Summary
The blog post explores the ambiguity surrounding the 'Design Engineer' job title, highlighting its emergence at the intersection of design and front-end development. It examines how AI tools are reshaping the role by blurring boundaries between design and engineering, enabling new workflows where non-engineers contribute directly to codebases. The author analyzes experiments at companies like Alan and Tracksuit, emphasizing challenges like technical fragility and trust-building, while arguing that roles are evolving toward scope-based responsibilities rather than rigid titles. The post concludes that the essence of a Design Engineer lies in end-to-end ownership and adaptability between design and technical execution.
