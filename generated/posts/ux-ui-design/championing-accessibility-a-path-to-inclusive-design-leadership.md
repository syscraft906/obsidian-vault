# Championing Accessibility: a Path to Inclusive Design Leadership

## AI Summary
The blog post emphasizes the critical role of accessibility in design leadership, advocating for a shift-left approach to integrate inclusivity from the start of projects. It highlights AI's potential in automating accessibility checks and enhancing user experiences through tools like screen readers and voice commands. Examples from Apple, Adobe, and partnerships with accessibility-focused companies illustrate practical implementations. The author calls on design leaders to champion accessibility as a moral imperative, not just a compliance task.
